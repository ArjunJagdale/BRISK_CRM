### Key Pages & Functionality

#### 1. Dashboard

A high-level overview for sales managers and team leads.

**Includes:**

* **KPI cards** showing total leads, converted leads, and other key metrics

  * Supports custom date range and preset duration filters
* **Leads by Source graph**

  * Visualizes lead generation from sources like Facebook Ads, Google Ads, etc.
* **Leads Over Time graph**

  * Tracks trends using custom and preset duration filters
* **Sales Team Performance section**

  * Highlights top-performing sales team members
* **Actionable Insights panel**

  * Displays high-priority updates and alerts related to leads and sales activities

---

#### 2. Lead Listing Page

A structured table view to manage all incoming leads.

**Displayed information:**

* Lead name, email, phone number
* Lead source
* Assigned sales member
* Last contacted date
* Created date
* Action options to manage each lead

---

#### 3. Lead Listing Filters

Advanced filtering options to help users narrow down leads efficiently.

**Filters include:**

* Lead status (New, Contacted, Scheduled, Declined, Converted)
* Lead source
* Assigned sales member
* Duration range
* Car-specific filters (Body Type, Company)
* **‚ÄúAll Car Filters‚Äù** for combined filtering

Users can also:

* Reassign a lead to a different sales member
* Update lead status
* Delete a lead (optional action)

---

#### 4. Car-Specific Filters

Dedicated filtering controls for automobile-based leads.

**Includes:**

* Body type dropdown
* Company dropdown
* **All Car Filters** option to filter by:

  * Body type
  * Company
  * Model

---

#### 5. Lead Details & Management

Accessible via the three-dot menu on each lead row.

**Options include:**

* **View Details**:
  Displays lead information in a card format
* **Manage Lead**:

  * Edit lead details
  * Mark as converted or declined
  * Contact via call or email
  * Schedule a test drive
  * Add or edit internal notes

---

### Design Focus

* Built for **real sales workflows**
* Emphasis on **speed, visibility, and reduced cognitive load**
* Clear separation between **overview (dashboard)** and **execution (lead management)**
